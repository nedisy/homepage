Make a suspension of kaolinite-rich clay in water, create a mist out of it, then heat or fire it at 850 degree Celsius until it is fully dehydrated. collect the dust, then it's ready for firing. After firing, the grinding process will be easier compared to grinding gravel-sized chunks.
